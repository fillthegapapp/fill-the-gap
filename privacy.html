This Privacy Policy applies only to information processed in connection with the Service and does not apply to third-party websites, applications, or services that may link to, from, or integrate with the Service, each of which is governed by its own terms and policies.

2) Information We May Collect

Depending on how you use the Service and which features you enable, we may collect or process the following categories of information:

  • Account and contact information. If you create an account, sign in, restore access, or contact support, we may process information such as your email address, account identifier, authentication-related information, and the contents of your support communications.
  • User-provided content and app inputs. We may process information you choose to provide through the Service, such as time selections, mood selections, saved preferences, custom entries, activity history, notes, favorites, or other information you voluntarily submit.
  • Device, diagnostics, and technical information. We may process information such as device type, operating system version, app version, language, approximate error state, crash information, diagnostics, and similar technical data when necessary to operate, secure, maintain, or troubleshoot the Service.
  • Identifiers. We may process internal account identifiers, app instance identifiers, device-associated identifiers, subscription identifiers, or similar identifiers reasonably necessary to authenticate users, maintain sessions, enforce entitlements, or protect the Service.
  • Purchase and subscription information. If you purchase a subscription through Apple, Apple processes the payment transaction. We may receive subscription status, entitlement status, transaction-related metadata, and restoration signals from Apple and from service providers that help us manage subscription access. We do not receive your full payment card number.

Certain information may be stored locally on your device rather than transmitted to our systems, depending on the architecture of the feature and your device settings.

Some app content, settings, logs, and feature state may be stored locally on your device as part of normal operation. Local storage may include, without limitation, saved preferences, activity history, app state, or other user-entered or user-generated information. Locally stored data may be removed if you delete the app, clear local storage, reset your device, or restore from a backup that does not include such data.

Local storage behavior may vary depending on your device, operating system, backup settings, and whether you use account-based or cloud-enabled functionality.
